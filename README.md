# Memory Card Game

A simple memory card game where you flip cards and try to find matching pairs. Built with just HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

---

## What it does

- Flip two cards at a time and find the matching emoji
- Tracks how many moves you've made and how long you've taken
- Timer starts when you flip your first card
- Three difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard
- Shows a win screen at the end with your score

---

## How to play

Open the live website here: [Try the Live Demo](https://memory-jade-eight.vercel.app/)

1. Click any card to flip it
2. Click another card to find its match
3. If they match, they stay flipped
4. If they don't, they flip back after a second
5. Match all pairs to win

---

## Difficulty levels

- **Easy** — 4x4 grid, 8 pairs. Good to start with
- **Medium** — 5x4 grid, 10 pairs. A bit harder
- **Hard** — 6x6 grid, 18 pairs. Actually hard

---

Just wanted to make a clean memory game without ads or pop-ups. Most browser games are full of that stuff.
